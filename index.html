Welcome! Here's how it works 👋

Follow these four steps to walk into every class feeling prepared and confident.

Step 1
Add Materials
Upload your readings, lecture notes, or paste key content so the AI can help you prepare.
Step 2
Pre-Class Prep
Chat with your AI tutor to understand key concepts and build your discussion position before class.
Step 3
Rehearsal
Practice speaking with AI classmates who ask questions and challenge your ideas out loud.
Step 4
Reflection & AI Phrases
Log how class went, get AI feedback, and collect discussion phrases tailored to your material.

You can always come back to your dashboard from the sidebar.
